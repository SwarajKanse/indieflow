Born From a Freelancer's Struggle

IndieFlow started with a simple, frustrating problem. Our founder, a freelance designer, was passionate about his creative work but constantly bogged down by financial admin. Creating GST invoices, tracking payments, and figuring out his taxes was a monthly nightmare.

He realized that the tools available were either too complex for a one-person business or too simple to be truly useful. The entire financial system seemed built for salaried employees, not for the growing army of independent professionals. That's when the idea for IndieFlow was born: a single, beautiful platform to handle all the financial complexities, so creators can focus on what they do best.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make, from the features we build to the way we support our users. They're not just words on a wall—they're the foundation of everything we do.

Creator-First

Our users are at the heart of everything. We build tools that solve their real-world problems and empower their success, not ours.

Beautifully Simple

We believe powerful software doesn't have to be complicated. We obsess over clean design and intuitive user experiences.

Radical Transparency

We are open and honest in our pricing, our security practices, and our communication. Trust is earned, not given.
